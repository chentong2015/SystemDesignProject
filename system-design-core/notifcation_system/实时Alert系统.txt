实战项目设计:
1. Design a notification service
2. Design a notification system for price alerts.
3. Design a near real time stock price alert system.
   You basically receive time series of stock prices and alert the user
   when the stock price 'crosses' the price they set in their alert.

Design Tips:
Receive prices over kafka, send the notifications over kafka,
then figure out how to optimize the alert query,
probably end up with some sort of in memory solution.
